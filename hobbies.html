Gaming

Gaming has always been the most relaxing outlet for me outside of school work, working out, or work in general. I play many different types of games and enjoy online competitive gaming. Gaming also played a major role in my introduction to software engineering. Through games I developed an interest in game design. After learning some game design through Unreal Engine 5, I wanted to learn about how to create functionality in a game without certain assets or tools already being there for me. This is the idea that brought me where I am today. I continue to play games today and am always looking for someone to play my favorite game with, which is League of Legends. If anyone is interested, add me as a friend! Check out my profile at op.gg.
